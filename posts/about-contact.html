About MoMA

The Modern Microprocessors Architectures (MoMA) Lab of New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) focuses on security and privacy of modern high-performance and embedded microprocessors. Research interests of the lab include privacy-preserving architectures, encrypted computation, industrial control systems security, and hardware security.
